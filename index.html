Ways I Can Help

From Strategy to Confidence on the Ground

The same plain-language, people-first approach that guides everyday people with their technology, applied to the challenges your business faces. No jargon. No unnecessary complexity. Just clear thinking and honest advice.

Business Analysis

Understanding how your business actually works before recommending anything. I map your processes, surface the gaps, and translate operational reality into clear technology requirements.

Executive Briefing

Helping leaders understand what a technology decision actually means, in plain language, with real business context. Vendor pitch, board paper, or a question from your team: I translate jargon into clarity.

Program Management

Keeping technology projects on the rails from the business side. Oversight that keeps stakeholders informed, timelines honest, and delivery teams focused on outcomes rather than outputs.

Digital Strategy

Helping your organisation decide where technology fits, not just what to buy or build. A coherent direction aligned with your actual goals, culture, and capacity to change.

Digital Literacy for Leaders and Staff

Practical workshops and briefings that build real confidence with technology, for leaders who need to make decisions and staff who need to work with new tools every day.

AI Adoption

Helping your organisation understand what AI can realistically do for your business, where to start, what to avoid, and how to bring your people along rather than leaving them behind.

Security Practices

Building a culture of sensible security, without the fear-mongering. Practical guidance on what your business actually needs to do to protect itself and the people it serves.

Change Management

Technology change is people change. I help organisations prepare their teams, manage the transition, and build the kind of buy-in that makes new technology stick rather than stall.
